# StepManiaX: Regional Expansion Strategy (Land and Expand)

**Objective:** Transition from a single-club pilot to a full-portfolio regional deployment within Planet Fitness Franchise Groups.

---

## 1. The Pilot Phase (Days 1-90)
*Goal: Prove Engagement and Gather Local ROI.*
- **Step 1:** Select a high-traffic location with a strong Gen Z/Millennial demographic.
- **Step 2:** Deploy a single All-In-One unit with automated usage tracking enabled.
- **Step 3:** Conduct weekly staff checks and monthly member feedback surveys via QR codes on the machine.

## 2. The Pilot ROI Presentation (Day 100)
*Goal: Secure Commitment for Wave 1 Expansion.*
- Present the `pilot-performance-report.md` to the Franchise Owner/COO.
- Highlight the **Retention Lift** metric—proving that the machine reduces cardio boredom.
- Propose a 5-club "Wave 1" expansion using tiered regional pricing or revenue-share models.

## 3. Wave 1 Expansion (Months 4-6)
*Goal: Prove Scalability and Operational Consistency.*
- Deploy StepManiaX units to 5 additional clubs across the regional portfolio.
- Standardize staff training using the `staff-training-manual.md`.
- Implement a centralized maintenance dashboard to track all 6 active units (including the pilot).

## 4. Full Regional Rollout (Months 7+)
*Goal: Complete Portfolio Saturation.*
- Leverage the data from 6 locations to trigger the "Land and Expand" volume discount clause in the commercial contract.
- Deploy units to all remaining locations in the franchisee's territory (e.g., all 50 EPIC Fitness locations in Michigan).
- Establish a long-term service and software license agreement with the manufacturer (Step Revolution).

---
## Key Success Metrics for Expansion
1. **Pilot Satisfaction Score:** > 8.5/10.
2. **Usage Frequency:** > 40 unique scans per day per unit.
3. **Retention Correlation:** Members who use SMX 2+ times/week show a 15% lower churn rate than the gym average.
