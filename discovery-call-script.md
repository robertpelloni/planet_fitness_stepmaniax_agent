# Discovery Call Script: StepManiaX Strategic Pilot

**Objective:** Secure a commitment for a 90-day, $0 hardware lease pilot at a priority location.
**Target Tone:** Professional, data-driven, and focused on operational efficiency.

---

## 1. Introduction & Context (1 Minute)
"Hi [Name], thanks for taking a few minutes today. As I mentioned in my note, we’re currently working with select Planet Fitness franchise groups to pilot a next-generation interactive cardio platform called StepManiaX.

The goal isn't just to add another machine to the floor, but to address a specific challenge we’re seeing across the industry: cardio boredom among Gen Z and Millennial members, and how that impacts long-term retention."

## 2. Discovery Questions (3-5 Minutes)
*Goal: Uncover pain points related to retention and floor utilization.*

- "Currently, how are you tracking member engagement on your cardio floor beyond simple check-ins?"
- "Have you noticed a shift in equipment preference among your younger demographic (Gen Z/Millennials) over the last 18-24 months?"
- "When members cancel, how often is 'boredom' or 'lack of engagement' cited as a factor in their exit surveys?"
- "Operational question: How much flexibility do your club managers currently have to experiment with new amenities or HIIT-focused zones?"

## 3. The Solution: StepManiaX (3 Minutes)
"StepManiaX is a commercial-grade 'Gamified HIIT' platform. It transforms high-intensity cardio into an interactive experience.

- **Engagement:** It provides instant feedback and progression, creating a 'flow state' that keeps members coming back.
- **Low Impact:** Our spring-loaded stages are designed for intense workouts with significantly less joint stress than traditional treadmills.
- **Etiquette Friendly:** It can be locked to 10-minute sessions and run via headphone jack to ensure it fits perfectly within the 'Judgement Free Zone'."

## 4. The "Rogue Pilot" Proposal (2 Minutes)
"We’re not asking for a capital expenditure today. We’re offering a **100% risk-mitigated, 90-day pilot**:

- **$0 Hardware Lease:** We provide the kiosk and stages at no cost.
- **Turnkey Service:** we handle installation and all maintenance with a 48-hour SLA.
- **Success Metric:** At the end of 90 days, we’ll provide you with a full ROI report on usage frequency and member engagement lift."

## 5. Closing & Next Steps (1 Minute)
"If I were to send over the 1-page MOU (Memorandum of Understanding) later today, would you be open to reviewing it with your operations lead to identify a potential pilot location?"

---
**Key Phrases to Use:**
- "Member Retention Lift"
- "Gamified HIIT"
- "Interactive Generation"
- "Zero-Risk Data Gathering"
- "Operational Leeway"

**Phrases to Avoid:**
- "Video Game"
- "Arcade"
- "Fun" (Use "Engaging" or "High-Intensity" instead)
- "Dance" (Use "Step-based HIIT" or "Coordination Training" instead)
