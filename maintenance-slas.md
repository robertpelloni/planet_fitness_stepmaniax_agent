# StepManiaX: Commercial Service Level Agreement (SLA)
**Pilot Program Maintenance & Reliability Standards**

This document details the technical reliability standards and service commitments provided by the StepManiaX B2B Initiative during the 90-day pilot program.

## 1. 48-Hour Service Guarantee
We understand that equipment uptime is critical for member satisfaction. During the pilot period, we commit to the following:
- **Response Time:** Any reported hardware or software issue will receive a technical response within 4 business hours.
- **Resolution Time:** On-site service, sensor calibration, or component replacement will be completed within **48 hours** of initial reporting, ensuring minimal disruption to the gym floor.

## 2. Technical Reliability Specifications
StepManiaX hardware is designed for the rigorous, 24/7 environment of commercial fitness centers.
- **Industrial-Grade Steel Construction:** The kiosk and stages are built with heavy-duty reinforced steel capable of withstanding constant 300lb+ impacts.
- **Redundant Load Cells:** Each stage utilizes industrial load cells (pressure sensors) with a MTBF (Mean Time Between Failure) of over 10 million activations.
- **Solid-State Computing:** The internal control unit is solid-state with no moving parts (excluding fans), significantly reducing heat-related failures.

## 3. Remote Monitoring & Updates
- **Automatic Sync:** Software updates, bug fixes, and new fitness content are pushed automatically via Wi-Fi/Ethernet during off-peak hours (e.g., 3:00 AM).
- **Proactive Health Checks:** Our team performs weekly remote health checks on sensor sensitivity and load cell calibration to address potential issues before they impact the user experience.

## 4. Sanitation & Hygiene
- **High-Durability Surfaces:** All contact surfaces are non-porous and designed to withstand standard gym-grade medical disinfectants.
- **Minimal Touchpoints:** The primary interface is a high-sensitivity touchscreen, minimizing physical button wear and facilitating easy cleaning.
