# Case Study: Autonomous B2B Sales Agent (StepManiaX & Planet Fitness)

## 1. Executive Summary
This project serves as a comprehensive case study in utilizing autonomous AI agents (Hermes-Agent/GPT-5.5) to execute a complete B2B sales pipeline. The target is the deployment of StepManiaX (SMX) commercial cardio units within the Planet Fitness franchise ecosystem.

## 2. The Challenge: The Corporate Monopoly
Planet Fitness operates under rigid, exclusive corporate procurement contracts. Direct entry via corporate is often blocked by multi-year exclusivity deals with legacy cardio manufacturers.

## 3. The Strategy: The "Rogue Franchise Loophole"
Rather than fighting corporate procurement, the agent identifies and targets **Regional Franchise Groups** (e.g., EPIC Fitness, National Fitness Partners). These groups possess:
- **Operational Leeway:** The autonomy to pilot new amenities to boost local club performance.
- **Urgent Pain Points:** Direct exposure to member churn and cardio boredom.
- **Rapid Decision Cycles:** Ability to approve 90-day pilots without corporate-level board reviews.

## 4. Technical Architecture
The project employs a hybrid three-pillar architecture:
1. **B2B Sales Collateral:** A suite of Markdown assets (MOU, SLA, FAQ, Scripts) that strictly enforce a professional, non-arcade tone.
2. **Lead Generation Automation:** Python scripts (`scrape_leads.py`) designed to identify regional powerhouses and executive decision-makers.
3. **Automation & Analytics:** Data-driven tools (`roi_calculator.py`, `generate_personalized_assets.py`) that turn lead data into financial projections and tailored outreach.

## 5. Tone Governance
A critical component of the case study is **Tone Management**. The agent is strictly instructed to strip away all "arcade" and "gaming" terminology, framing the hardware instead as "Next-Generation Gamified HIIT." This minimizes friction with corporate gym standards.

## 6. Conclusion: The Zero-Risk Pilot
The ultimate goal is the **Zero-Risk Pilot Framework**—a $0 hardware lease for 90 days. This removes financial risk from the franchisee and allows the agent to prove ROI through real-world usage data, triggering long-term commercial contracts.
