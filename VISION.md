# Vision Document

## Long-term Product Goal
The long-term goal of this project is twofold:
1. Successfully persuade Planet Fitness to deploy StepManiaX (SMX) commercial units within their locations, transforming tedious cardio workouts into high-engagement, gamified High-Intensity Interval Training (HIIT) experiences.
2. Persuade and influence industry leaders (Konami, StepManiaX, Andamiro) to implement 1+ hour long "Marathon Cardio" psytrance sets, utilizing "AutoArrow" machine-learning generated stepfiles for all difficulty levels to support sustained, flow-state endurance training.

## The Two-Path Strategic Model
Our approach operates on two converging paths designed to force adoption within the highly restricted corporate fitness ecosystem:

### Path A: The Rogue Franchise Loophole (Regional)
Bypassing the rigid and impenetrable corporate monopoly by targeting regional Franchise Group owners who have the operational leeway to experiment with amenities. We offer a $0 lease pilot, gather hardcore retention data, and secure regional procurement contracts.

### Path B: The Corporate Vendor Pipeline (National)
A direct engagement with Planet Fitness corporate procurement, fueled by the data generated in Path A. This path transitions from off-the-shelf hardware to a **proprietary 9-panel commercial fitness stage manufactured by Andamiro**, running a custom fitness-focused fork of the StepMania engine.

## Design Direction
The approach is strictly B2B and commercial. We must strip away arcade and gaming terminology, instead framing the platform as a highly durable, zero-risk, high-retention fitness tool.

### Key Strategic Pillars:
1. **Rogue Franchise Loophole:** Target regional owners to build a bulletproof data case.
2. **Custom Hardware Integration:** Partner with Andamiro to build a 9-panel matrix designed purely for biomechanical HIIT.
3. **Software Decoupling:** Fork StepMania to provide a medical/commercial grade UI (Heart Rate, METs) entirely devoid of arcade aesthetics.
4. **Data-Driven Upsell:** Use pilot metrics (scans per day, session duration) to justify national corporate vendor approval.
5. **Autonomous Management:** Leverage background automation for lead follow-up, health monitoring, and engagement tracking to reduce human overhead.
6. **Content Innovation:** Continuously deploy ML-generated "Marathon Content" to sustain member flow states.
