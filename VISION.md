# Vision Document

## Long-term Product Goal
The long-term goal of this project is twofold:

1. **Persuade Planet Fitness to deploy custom fitness rhythm hardware** within their locations — transforming tedious cardio workouts into high-engagement, gamified High-Intensity Interval Training (HIIT) experiences — via direct corporate engagement, regional franchise pilots, or a combined two-path strategy.

2. **Partner with Andamiro to design and manufacture a custom 9-panel fitness rhythm stage**, running a proprietary StepMania-based fitness software stack with ML-generated marathon content, positioning both companies as leaders in the $96B commercial fitness market.

## Strategic Architecture: The Two-Path Model

Rather than a single approach, the project pursues two parallel paths that feed each other:

### Path A — Regional Franchise Pilots (Existing "Rogue Loophole")
*Target: Regional franchise groups with operational autonomy.*

- Deploy existing StepManiaX commercial units (or custom prototypes) under zero-risk 90-day leases.
- Collect real-world retention, session-duration, and member-engagement data.
- Use pilot data as ammunition for Path B and as proof-of-concept for Andamiro's custom hardware.

### Path B — Corporate + Manufacturer Partnership (New Vertical)
*Target: Planet Fitness corporate procurement + Andamiro hardware division.*

- Commission Andamiro to produce a custom 9-panel fitness rhythm stage — purpose-built for gym environments, not arcades.
- Develop a custom StepMania-based fitness operating system with a commercial-grade UI, heart-rate zone integration, and marathon content across all difficulty levels.
- Present a unified hardware + software + content solution to Planet Fitness corporate as a new equipment category (not an arcade game).

## Design Direction

The approach is strictly B2B and commercial. We must strip away arcade and gaming terminology, instead framing the platform as a highly durable, zero-risk, high-retention fitness tool.

The ultimate end-state is a **vertically integrated product**: custom hardware by Andamiro, custom software by this project, infinite ML-generated content via AutoArrow, and a direct corporate supply agreement with Planet Fitness.

### Key Strategic Pillars:
1. **Rogue Franchise Loophole:** Target regional owners (e.g., EPIC Fitness Group, NFP) who have the autonomy to pilot new amenities — generating real-world data to de-risk the corporate pitch.
2. **Custom Hardware Partnership:** Commission Andamiro to engineer a purpose-built 9-panel fitness stage with industrial-grade load cells, medical-grade surfaces, and a form factor designed for commercial gym floors, not arcades.
3. **Fitness-Native Software:** Fork StepMania into a fitness-first operating system with workout-mode UI, heart-rate telemetry, MET tracking, and a "cardio session" paradigm instead of "game rounds."
4. **Infinite ML Content:** Leverage the AutoArrow ML Engine to generate beat-synchronized, difficulty-scaled marathon content (60-120 minute Psytrance sets) at every skill level — eliminating the content ceiling that limits traditional rhythm platforms.
5. **Data-Driven Upsell:** Use pilot metrics (scans per day, session duration, retention lift) to justify long-term commercial contracts and national vendor approval.
6. **Autonomous Orchestration:** Leverage background automation for lead follow-up, health monitoring, engagement tracking, and report generation to reduce human overhead across both paths.

## Success Criteria

### Phase 1 — Technical Validation
- [ ] Andamiro agrees to engineering feasibility study for a custom 9-panel production run.
- [ ] StepMania fitness fork boots on reference hardware with custom UI.
- [ ] 9-panel input driver functional with load-cell calibration.
- [ ] AutoArrow generates a verified 60-minute marathon chart at all 5 difficulty levels.

### Phase 2 — Market Validation
- [ ] At least 3 regional franchise pilots active with real member usage data.
- [ ] Positive retention lift trend (≥15% session duration increase vs. traditional cardio).
- [ ] Planet Fitness corporate procurement engages in exploratory discussions.

### Phase 3 — Commercial Deployment
- [ ] Andamiro produces a first production batch of custom 9-panel units.
- [ ] Planet Fitness corporate approves a pilot installation in select corporate-owned locations.
- [ ] National vendor agreement signed for ongoing supply and support.
