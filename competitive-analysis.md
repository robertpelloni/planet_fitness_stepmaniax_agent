## 3. The Planet Fitness "Judgement Free" Fit
Traditional exergaming can sometimes be intimidating or "performative." StepManiaX addresses this for the PF environment:
- **Headphone-Only Audio:** Eliminates "arcade noise" from the gym floor.
- **Adjustable Difficulty:** Ensures a 60-year-old beginner feels as welcome as a 20-year-old athlete.
- **Cardio-First Framing:** Positioned as a **High-Intensity Interval Training (HIIT)** tool, not a game.

## 4. Economic Competitive Advantage
- **Zero-Risk Entry:** Most commercial cardio requires 3-5 year lease commitments upfront. StepManiaX pilots are **$0 for 90 days**.
- **Data Transparency:** Unlike standard treadmills, we provide granular usage data (unique scans, session length) to prove ROI before you buy.
