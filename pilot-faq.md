# StepManiaX: Pilot FAQ for Club Managers

This FAQ is designed to address club-level operational questions during the setup and execution of the 90-day StepManiaX pilot.

---

## 1. Operational & Floor Layout
**Q: How much space does the unit require?**
A: The standard All-In-One configuration requires a footprint of approximately 75"W x 95"D. We recommend a high-visibility area in your functional training or cardio zone.

**Q: Does it require special power?**
A: No. It runs on a standard 120V outlet. We recommend a dedicated circuit if possible, but it is not required.

**Q: Is it loud? Will it disturb the gym floor?**
A: No. The unit can be configured for "Silent Mode," where audio is delivered exclusively through a 3.5mm headphone jack or Bluetooth sync. The mechanical sound of the stages is comparable to a standard treadmill.

## 2. Member Etiquette & Usage
**Q: How do we prevent members from 'hogging' the machine?**
A: The software includes an "Operator Mode" where we can set a strict session limit (e.g., 2 songs or 10 minutes). Once the limit is reached, the user must step off to allow others to scan in.

**Q: Is it too difficult for beginners?**
A: Not at all. There are over 5 levels of difficulty. For the pilot, we can lock out the "Extreme" tiers to ensure the focus remains on accessible HIIT cardio for all fitness levels.

**Q: Can it be used by members with physical limitations?**
A: Yes. The stages are spring-loaded and shock-absorbing, making it a lower-impact alternative to traditional running. The handrails provide additional stability for users who require it.

## 3. Maintenance & Support
**Q: What happens if it breaks?**
A: As part of the pilot, we retain full service ownership. Any hardware issues reported to our support line will be addressed on-site within 48 hours.

**Q: How do we clean the unit?**
A: The unit is designed for commercial environments. The touchscreens and metal stages can be cleaned using the same medical-grade disinfectants already used in your club.

**Q: Does it require Wi-Fi?**
A: Wi-Fi or Ethernet is preferred for automatic software updates and usage data syncing, but the unit is fully functional offline.

## 4. Pilot Objectives
**Q: Why are we doing this pilot?**
A: To track member engagement and retention lift. We want to see if "Gamified HIIT" reduces cardio boredom and increases the frequency of member visits.
