# Partnership Proposal: Next-Generation Commercial Fitness Platform

**To:** Andamiro International - Corporate Procurement & Engineering
**From:** StepManiaX Commercial Fitness Initiative
**Date:** June 2026
**Subject:** Joint Development of a Custom 9-Panel Commercial Fitness Exergaming Platform

## Executive Summary
We are proposing a strategic manufacturing partnership with Andamiro to produce a custom, industrial-grade 9-panel fitness stage. This hardware will serve as the foundation for a proprietary B2B fitness software platform currently being piloted with regional franchise operators of major global gym chains (e.g., Planet Fitness). By combining Andamiro's world-class arcade manufacturing supply chain with our specialized marathon-cardio software, we can unlock a multi-million dollar corporate fitness market entirely insulated from traditional amusement industry fluctuations.

## Why Andamiro?
Andamiro possesses the most advanced, proven supply chain for high-impact step-based exergaming hardware (Pump It Up). Your historical willingness to experiment with new form factors and content makes you the ideal partner to bridge the gap between amusement hardware and medical/commercial fitness equipment.

## The Hardware Specification: Custom 9-Panel Matrix
We are requesting a Feasibility Study and subsequent production of a custom stage with the following deviations from standard PIU hardware:
- **Layout:** 3x3 (9-panel) uniform grid.
- **Sensors:** Transition from standard microswitches to highly calibrated load cells for dynamic weight tracking and durability under 24/7 commercial gym abuse.
- **Materials:** Industrial steel frame, medical-grade non-slip polycarbonate panel surfaces, and sweat-resistant sealed electronics.
- **Feedback:** Integrated LED array synced via custom SPI ADC interface.

## Target Economics & Production
- **Target BOM/Unit Cost:** ~$6,000 USD at scale.
- **Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ):** Targeting an initial run of 500 units upon successful completion of the regional Phase 1 pilot.
- **Competitive Advantage:** Standard commercial treadmills cost between $8,000 - $15,000. Our platform offers superior member retention metrics at a substantially lower capital expenditure for gym owners.

## The Pilot Path to Production
1. **Q3 2026:** Fund and initiate a Mechanical Feasibility Study for the 9-panel matrix.
2. **Q4 2026:** Produce a 5-unit prototype run for live stress testing in regional pilot clubs.
3. **Q1 2027:** Execute UL, CE, and ADA compliance certification blueprints based on the prototypes.
4. **Q2 2027:** Initiate the first 500-unit commercial production run backed by a corporate commitment letter from the pilot franchise groups.

## Revenue Share Model
- **Andamiro:** Retains primary margin on all hardware manufacturing and fulfillment.
- **Our Initiative:** Retains revenue from the proprietary B2B software license, API telemetry access, and the continuous "Marathon Content" subscription model.

We look forward to discussing the engineering feasibility and the financial mechanics of this partnership.
