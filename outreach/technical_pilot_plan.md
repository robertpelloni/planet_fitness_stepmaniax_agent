# 90-Day Technical Pilot Demonstration Plan

## Objective
To execute a fully measured, 90-day technical demonstration of StepManiaX hardware in a live regional Planet Fitness setting, validating member engagement, equipment durability, and operational telemetry through our B2B CRM.

## Phase 1: Deployment & Calibration (Days 1-14)
* **Objective:** Install hardware, ensure network connectivity, and establish baseline telemetry.
* **Key Actions:**
    * Deploy 1x StepManiaX commercial unit to the designated Franchise Group location.
    * Install the `health_monitor.py` heartbeat integration to stream uptime and scan metrics directly to the Command Center.
    * Provide local staff with minimal training to test the "Zero-Friction" onboarding hypothesis.

## Phase 2: Engagement & Telemetry Harvesting (Days 15-60)
* **Objective:** Monitor member usage, retention rates, and peak usage hours using the Live Ops Wallboard.
* **Key Actions:**
    * Activate the `/api/v1/analytics/live-occupancy` endpoint to track session intensity and frequency.
    * Identify top users via NFC/Biometric check-ins (simulated or real) and record session lengths.
    * Ensure the `launch_outreach.py` orchestrator sends weekly summary reports to the Franchise Manager.

## Phase 3: "Flow State" Marathon Trial (Days 61-75)
* **Objective:** Introduce long-form (Psytrance) content generated by the AutoArrow ML Prototype to test sustained cardio endurance and member flow state.
* **Key Actions:**
    * Push 3-5 ML-generated tracks to the unit via over-the-air updates (simulated in the CRM pipeline).
    * Measure the shift in average session duration (e.g., from 15 minutes to 35+ minutes).
    * Collect Member Feedback through the Prospect Portal survey forms.

## Phase 4: Data Synthesis & Commercial Conversion (Days 76-90)
* **Objective:** Package the 90-day telemetry into a comprehensive ROI report to trigger commercial scaling.
* **Key Actions:**
    * Generate the Final Pilot Performance Report comparing baseline treadmill usage to SMX utilization.
    * Present the interactive ROI simulator data to Franchise executives.
    * Execute the `convert_lead_to_partner` action within the Admin CRM to officially transition the pilot location into a portfolio account.

## Success Metrics
1. **Uptime:** > 99.5% hardware reliability over 90 days.
2. **Engagement:** > 200 unique check-ins per week.
3. **Retention Lift:** A measurable (2-4%) localized retention lift amongst active SMX users compared to general membership.
