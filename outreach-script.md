## 2. Overcoming Immediate Objections (Agent Cheat Sheet)

During discovery calls or follow-up communications, be armed with these direct counters to common franchise owner objections:

**Objection:** *"It’s too noisy/disruptive."*
**Counter:** The unit can be configured to run exclusively via a standard 3.5mm headphone jack or Bluetooth sync, keeping the floor completely silent.

**Objection:** *"People will hog it / intimidate beginners."*
**Counter:** The software can be locked to a strict 2-song or 10-minute maximum limit per scan/session, enforcing standard cardio floor etiquette. We can also lock out ultra-high difficulty tiers to keep the focus on casual fitness.

**Objection:** *"What if it breaks?"*
**Counter:** As part of the pilot, we retain service ownership. We establish a direct SLA with the manufacturer and handle any hardware updates or sensor calibrations within 48 hours at no cost to the club.
