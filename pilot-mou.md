## 1. Purpose
This Memorandum of Understanding establishes the framework for a 90-day, risk-free pilot placement of a StepManiaX (SMX) commercial cardio kiosk. The objective is to track member usage data, scan frequency, and retention lift to evaluate the potential for a broader regional rollout.

## 2. Term
The pilot period will last for **90 days** from the date of installation at the designated pilot location. Upon conclusion, both parties hold a mutual option to either extend the placement, negotiate a regional procurement contract, or remove the unit at the Provider's expense.

## 3. Cost & Requirements
- **Hardware Lease:** $0. The equipment is provided to the Franchisee at no cost for the duration of the pilot.
- **Facility Requirements:** The Franchisee agrees to provide standard 120V power and the necessary floor space (configurations require up to 75"W x 95"D) in a highly visible cardio or functional fitness area.

## 4. Maintenance & Liability
- **Service Ownership:** The Provider retains full service ownership. Any hardware updates, sensor calibrations, or technical issues will be resolved within 48 hours at no cost to the club.
- **Insurance:** The equipment is fully insured under a commercial general liability policy provided by the Provider. This policy indemnifies the Franchisee against any claims arising from software or hardware defects.

## 5. Data Sharing & Engagement Tracking
The Franchisee grants the Provider permission to place a non-intrusive QR code or digital check-in interface on the machine. This will be used strictly to track member satisfaction, engagement length, and usage frequency to compile an accurate ROI report at the end of the 90-day term.
